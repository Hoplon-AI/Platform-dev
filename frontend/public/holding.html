How it works

Three steps to underwriting-ready evidence

From raw documents to structured, source-linked risk evidence — with a clear, verified audit trail at every step.

01

Upload your portfolio

Drop in your Statement of Value (SOV) spreadsheet. EquiRisk validates, parses and builds your full property and block structure — typically in under 60 seconds.

02

Attach risk evidence

Upload FRA and FRAEW PDFs. Every page is read and structured — risk indicators, ratings, cladding types and remediation actions, each tied back to its source.

03

Export ready reports

Download Doc A (per-unit schedule) and Doc B (per-block risk summary) — enriched, structured, and ready for underwriting submission.
